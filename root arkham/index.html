About The Game

Arkham Horror is a co-op card game based on Lovecraftian Horror. Each player will control an investigator with unique abilities and customize a deck to help them navigate the horrors to come.

The player(s) pick a campagin to play through that can be played in one sitting or over a couple of games. They will be forced to make difficult choices that will impact the results of future scenarios.

1-2 Hours

1+ Players

Ages 14+
